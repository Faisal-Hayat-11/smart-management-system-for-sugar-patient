Dietary Recommendation for Sugar Paitent

High-Fiber Foods

Choose whole grains like oats, quinoa, and whole wheat. Non-starchy vegetables like spinach, kale, and broccoli are great choices due to their high fiber content and low glycemic index.

Lean Proteins

Opt for fish (salmon, sardines) and lean poultry. Beans, legumes, tofu, and low-fat dairy are protein-rich and help control blood sugar.

Low-Glycemic Fruits

Enjoy berries (blueberries, strawberries), apples, and pears in moderate portions. Pair them with protein or healthy fats to prevent spikes.

Hydration and Beverages

Stay hydrated with water and unsweetened herbal teas. Avoid sugary drinks and try infused water with lemon or cucumber as a refreshing alternative.

Portion Control and Timing

Eat small, frequent meals for stable blood sugar. Balance meals with protein, fiber, and healthy fat for a slow and even digestion.
